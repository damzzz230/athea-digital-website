## 3. The Core USP — The Spec-Build Model

This is Athea Digital's single biggest differentiator and must be communicated clearly in all content.

> **We build the site first. You see it, love it, then you pay.**

Most agencies ask for a deposit and a signed contract before doing any work. Athea does the opposite:
- They build the full site upfront based on the client's business type and niche.
- The client sees the completed site before committing to anything.
- If they love it, they agree on a price and pay before handover.
- If something isn't right, they iterate until it is.
- **No deposits. No upfront risk to the client.**

This model reflects their confidence in the quality of their work and means they only build sites they genuinely believe in.

## 5. Services & What They Deliver

All sites are custom-built (not drag-and-drop builders like Wix or Squarespace). Key deliverables included across packages:

- Mobile-first responsive design (80%+ of local searches are on mobile)
- WhatsApp CTA buttons (primary contact method in South Africa)
- Google Maps embeds and location/directions links
- Conversion-focused layouts (CTAs, copy, structure all optimised)
- Photo/work galleries
- Contact forms
- Basic on-page SEO
- Cloudflare hosting setup
- Fast, fixed delivery (full build delivered within a 2–3 week lead time)

**Niche-specific extras include:**
- Online booking integrations (Hair Salons)
- Digital menus with photos (Restaurants)
- Emergency call CTAs (Trades)
- Class schedules and lead capture (Fitness)
- Filterable portfolio galleries (Tattoo Studios)
- Package pricing with before/after gallery (Auto Detailing)

## 8. Brand Voice & Tone

The Athea Digital brand voice is:

- **Confident, not arrogant** — they know their work is good, but they let it speak for itself
- **Direct and no-fluff** — short, punchy sentences; no waffle
- **Conversational but professional** — feels like talking to a skilled freelancer, not a corporate agency
- **Local and relatable** — speaks like a Joburg business person, not a Silicon Valley pitch
- **Results-focused** — always ties back to what the client actually gets (bookings, leads, customers)

**Headline examples from the site:**
- "We Build Websites That Win Clients."
- "Built Different. Delivered Faster."
- "Flat rates. No surprises."
- "Two developers. One obsession."
- "Let's build something that works."
- "We build it first. You love it, then you pay."

**Avoid:**
- Corporate jargon ("synergies", "leverage", "solutions")
- Overpromising ("best in the world", "guaranteed results")
- Lengthy, passive sentences
- Anything that sounds like a big agency
